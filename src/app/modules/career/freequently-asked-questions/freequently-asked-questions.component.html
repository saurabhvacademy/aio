Frequently Asked Questions

  • The ExitTest Qualifier Test (EQT) is a General Mental Ability Test to assess candidates for their suitability for a career in the corporate world. The assessment is done in the following areas:
    • Behavioural Competencies (65 Qs in 15 Mins): This section will have questions to test behavioural competencies of a candidate on the following: Accountability, Learning Approach, Ambition, Interpersonal Skills, Emotional Stability, Team-Work, Influencing Skills & Prudence. This section will contain statements related to typical behaviors. Read each statement carefully and try to answer it quickly, do not spend too much time on any one question. There are no right or wrong answers in psychometric questions. Describe yourself as honestly and accurately as possible. Please remember that your first response is probably the best.
    • Cognitive Skills (40 Qs in 10 Mins): This section will contain questions of a general nature in the following areas: Communication, Problem Solving & Attention to Detail
    • General Aptitude (30 Qs in 20 Mins): This section will test the general aptitude of the candidates in the following areas: Logical Reasoning, Financial Reasoning, Verbal Reasoning, Spatial Reasoning. The questions will assess English Grammar, Word Usage, General Mental Ability, Elementary Arithmetic, DI, Basic Statistics etc.
  • The test will have 135 Qs to be attempted within a duration of 45 minutes. Each candidate who completes the test will receive a composite score based on his answers to the questions in the test. This score will be used to shortlist candidates for next rounds of the recruitment process. The score is applicable across all the industries for entry level positions.
  • Note: The test is conducted in English and there will be no negative marking.
  • The EQT is a test of General Mental Ability which many companies use to either shortlist or to select candidates. By securing a high score in the EQT you demonstrate competencies which prospective employers look for in candidates. The EQT also helps companies to screen quality candidates from a large number of applicants thereby making their recruitment process easier and smoother.
  • To apply for the EQT you should meet the following criteria:

    • Students with any degree and specialisation/discipline
    • Freshers with less than 2 years of experience
