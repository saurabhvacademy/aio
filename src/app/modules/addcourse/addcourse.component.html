Are you sure you want to delete this course?

When you delete your course, you won’t be able to have an access to it again and you won’t be able to retrieve the content and information that you have shared on your course.

What happens if you delete your course?

  • 1.1 You won’t be able to have an access to your course again.
  • 1.2 Your photos, videos, course content and everything else that you have added will be permanently deleted. You won’t be able to retrieve anything that you have added.
  • 1.3You will lose people who are interested in purchasing and downloading your course.
  • 1.4Your course won’t be visible to anybody for purchase or download.

Are you sure to Un-publish this course?

Once you Un-publish your course, it will be no longer visible to anybody on Study24x7.

What happens after un-publishing the course?

  • 1.1 You will still have access to your course and its content.
  • 1.2 Your photos, videos, course content and everything else that you have added to your course will not be visible to anybody on Study24x7.
  • 1.3 You can lose your potential buyers who might be interested in purchasing your course.
  • 1.4Your course won’t be visible to anybody for purchase or download.
